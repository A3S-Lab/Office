# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Assume good intent while addressing concrete impact.
- Respect different levels of experience.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work.
- Protect private and security-sensitive information.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, and
publishing another person's private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to an A3S Lab maintainer through GitHub.
Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation,
or take other appropriate action. Reports will be handled as confidentially as
practical.
